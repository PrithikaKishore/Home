Paneer Pakoda

This is a very simple recipe & kids can do it by themselves.


INGREDIENTS:

  • Paneer
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Mayonise
  • Ketchup
  • Maida
  • Cornflaxes (OPTIONAL)
  • Chilly Powder
  • Mixed herbs (OPTIONAL)


PROCEDURE:

  1. Take maida in a bowl , add chilly powder & salt a mix well.
  2. Add the onion powder, pepper & mixed herbs.
  3. Add water little by little & mix well until it forms a thick batter.
  4. Now add all the paneer pieces and coat it well with the batter.
  5. IF YOU WANT YOU CAN COAT THE PIECES IN THE CORNFLAXES TO GET A CRISPY COATING. IT WILL TASTE AMAZING EVEN WITHOUT THIS.
  6. Add oil in a frying pan and heat it.
  7. Add the paneer pieces one by one & fry until it becomes golden brown in colour.

  8. Serve it with ur favourite dip.

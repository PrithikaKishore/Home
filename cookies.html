Ingredients

  • Maida- 100gms
  • Corn flour- 30gms
  • Salt- a pinch
  • Coffee Powder- 10gms
  • HotWater- 2tsp
  • Softened Butter- 60gms
  • Powdered sugar- 40gms


Preparation

  1. Sieve maida, cornflour & salt
  2. In a bowl add coffee powder & hot water and mix well.

  3. In a bowl add softened Butter. Beat for 2 mins
  4. Add the powdered sugar & miz until it becomes light & fluffy.

  5. Now add the dry mixtures in two batches and mix well (Till it looks like a crumb texture).
  6. Now add the coffee decoction
  7. Roll out the dough in about 4-5 mm thickness & cut to desired shape.

  8. STOVE: Low flame 15-20 mins
  9. OVEN: 180 degree celcius for 15-20 mins
AND... It's ready!üíï
